  • Gender Affirming Surgery Experience Survey
    If you’ve had a GA surgery, please consider submitting an anonymous report on your experience using this survey.

    There is a shortage of information about GA offerings, especially locally, and it can be hard to even find out what is available, much less what to expect in terms of experience, results, and cost. Using reports gathered from the above survey, we’ll create a database of these surgical experiences, which we’ll spread word of mouth through trans group chats & affinity groups and may possibly publish on our website, for prospective surgery patients to peruse at their leisure.
