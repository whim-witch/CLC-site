The Cobra Lilies are an anticapitalist mutual aid collective that brings trans people of the Upper Valley together to provide care for each other. We hold a biweekly support group, share skills, host trainings, and help each other access healthcare, clothing, food, and resources in our community.

Our purpose is not only to provide aid, but to build a support network and help each other grow and thrive so that we can struggle together against the systemic forces that oppress us. We believe our liberation will not come from above; it will come from a lateral resistance led for and by trans people.
